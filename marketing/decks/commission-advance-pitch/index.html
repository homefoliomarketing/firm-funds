02 / 10 · Problem
The problem

Your agents need money now. You have no safe way to give it to them.

Agents float closing costs out of pocket

Marketing, staging, listing fees, and personal bills pile up between firm and close. Top producers carry thousands in receivables every month with nothing coming in.

They ask you for a loan, or quietly leave

Agents go to high-interest factoring companies, max out cards, or move to a brokerage that offers an advance program. Either way, you lose.

Lending it yourself means real risk

To run an advance program in-house: tie up capital, build software, train staff, manage FINTRAC registration, file PPSA security, run a trust account, handle remediation when deals fall through. That is a whole second business.

Doing nothing is the silent cost

Recruiting suffers, retention suffers, and the brokerages that solve this win the next conversation. Agents talk to each other.

Compliance and trust

The regulated parts? Off your plate.

Lending to agents touches finance regulations the average brokerage is not set up for. Firm Funds carries every registration, filing, and account in-house, as part of the partnership.

FINTRAC registration

Security filings

Every advance is secured against the agent's pending commission through a PPSA registration. Filed, maintained, and discharged by us.
